WHAT WE DO

ZIM provides its customers with a comprehensive range of value added solid dosage differentiated generic products in semi-finished and finished categories / formulations. ZIM is now a Three Star Export House Certified Company.

ZIM Laboratories Limited has been certified as a Three Star Export House Company by Foreign Trade Policy, 2015-2020. The certificate is valid for the time span of five years, i.e., from 29th July 2017 to 28th July 2021. ZIM & NMIMS, Shirpur jointly organized a two days workshop on QbD & provided Hands on training on DOE on 6th & 7th October '17. The objective of the workshop was to focus on understanding a systematic approach to pharmaceutical development that is defined by Quality-by-Design principles, comprehending quality risk management, inculcating risk based thinking, an understanding of the product & process and using it to establish a control strategy as a part of the comprehensive quality risk management program, learning design expert software and its efficient use in pharmaceutical development.

ZIM Laboratories Ltd. received an award for 'Excellence in R&D' by UBM

ZIM received the Winner for the 'Excellence in R&D – Cost/Functional Improvement' award, the prestigious award was presented at a grand event with Industry leaders & visionaries from across the world in attendance on Monday, 27th November '17 at The Westin, Mumbai. The award was presented by UBM as a part of UBM India Pharma Awards '17, celebrating the excellence of Pharma innovators and champions from various verticals. Dr. Anwar Daud, Managing Director & Mrs. Nidhi Sapkal, Principal Research Coordinator humbly received the award on behalf of everyone at ZIM.
