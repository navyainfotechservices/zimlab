The facility has been accredited with approvals from EU-GMP, WHO-GMP and ISO 9001.

The plant is a dedicated facility to manufacture oral solid dosage forms like tablets, capsules, pellets, DC granules and powder for oral-suspension category products.

It has been constructed with adequate separation to cater to the activities required to manufacture different formulations, warehousing and quality control.

Over 260 personnel are employed at the general manufacturing facility with over 120 belonging to the QA and QC department.

The state-of-the-art OTF manufacturing facility is capable of manufacturing 324 million strips per annum.

This is a dedicated facility for manufacturing of OTF in various treatment areas and has separate nutraceutical and pharmaceutical manufacturing areas.

The OTF facility has a separate QA/QC lab along with separate warehouses for raw materials & packing materials.

There are currently 17 personnel employed in the OTF facility.

Occupying an area around 16,000 Sq.Ft, this separate formulation block is dedicated to the manufacturing of tablets, capsules and powder for the 'oral suspension of cephalosporin' category.

The Cephalosporin facility manufactures Taste Masked Pre-Formulation and Formulation products which are supplied to international customers.

The facility has been accredited with approvals from WHO-GMP and ISO 9001.

The two storeyed facility has a separate warehousing and quality-control area. The manufacturing operations are on the second floor with warehousing and QC on the ground floor.

It employs over 50 personnel in Cephalosporin facility.
